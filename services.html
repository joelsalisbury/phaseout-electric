Our technicians offer O&M of existing solar systems in Massachussetts and Connecticut. Our techs are all licensed and professionally trained on all aspects of elecrical service. We diagnose the problem the first trip. We are committed to resolving solar issues quickly to make sure your company is meeting its production guarantees. (This is pretty important in solar because these big companies are required buy law to produce a certain amount of power contractually.

Owner Adam Golka has been working in the electrical trade since 2000. He possesses vast experience in commercial and residential wiring in many different facets of the trade including:

  • Solar installation and maintenance
  • Network Cabling
  • Audio Visual
  • Fire and Access Control Cystems
  • Explosion-Proof Wiring for Gas Stations
  • Environmental Remediation Control Wiring
  • Residential Electric Serices
  • Service and Distribution Equipment
